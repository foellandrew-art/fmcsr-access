# FMCSR Driver Compliance Portal
### School Bus Edition — Digital Acknowledgment System

This repository hosts the official digital access point for the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSR) Pocketbook. It is designed to provide drivers with immediate access to required safety regulations and generate a verifiable receipt for their Driver Qualification (DQ) file.

## 📋 Purpose
Under FMCSR guidelines, motor carriers must ensure drivers are familiar with specific safety regulations. This portal streamlines that process by:
1. Providing direct links to the **Electronic Code of Federal Regulations (eCFR)**.
2. Capturing a digital signature/acknowledgment from the driver.
3. Generating a standardized, printable receipt for compliance records.

## 🔗 Regulatory Sections Covered
Drivers are required to review the following parts via this portal:
* **Part 390-391:** General Applicability & Driver Qualifications
* **Part 392 & 383.123:** Driving Rules & School Bus Endorsements
* **Part 395:** Hours of Service (Passenger Vehicle Limits)
* **Part 396:** Inspection & Maintenance
* **Part 40 & 382:** Drug & Alcohol Testing Procedures

## 🛠 Usage Instructions
1. **Access:** Navigate to the live GitHub Pages URL.
2. **Review:** Click the regulatory links to review current eCFR data.
3. **Sign:** Enter your full legal name in the Acknowledgment section.
4. **Generate:** Click "Sign and Generate Receipt."
5. **File:** Use the "Print for File" button to save as a PDF or print a hard copy for the Carrier Representative to countersign.

## 💻 Technical Details
* **Framework:** Single Page Application (SPA)
* **Styling:** Tailwind CSS (via CDN)
* **Deployment:** Hosted via GitHub Pages
* **Privacy:** This version processes signatures locally; no data is stored on a server. (Manual filing required).
