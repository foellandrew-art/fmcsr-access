I acknowledge receipt of this **FEDERAL MOTOR CARRIER SAFETY REGULATIONS POCKETBOOK (School Bus Edition)** via digital access. In addition, I agree to familiarize myself with the Federal Motor Carrier Safety Regulations (FMCSR) of the U.S. Department of Transportation, Parts 40, 380, 382, 383, 387, 390-397, and 399 of Subchapter B, Chapter III, Title 49 of the Code of Federal Regulations, as contained therein.
